# Stories Healthy Retention

## What was added
- Pinned story categories
- Stronger recent reading surfaces
- Recommended next stories
- Weekly reflection summary
- Stories shell and reader retention blocks

## Why
The stories section now encourages calm, healthy re-visits through continuity and relevance:
- continue reading
- pinned categories
- recent stories
- recommended next story
- weekly reflection

## Notes
- This phase does not add audio narration.
- This phase does not add OS-level reminders.
- Reader flow remains simple and offline-friendly.
