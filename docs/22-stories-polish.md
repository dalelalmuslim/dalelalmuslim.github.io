# Stories Polish Pass

This pass refines the Stories section visually without changing its storage contracts.

Highlights:
- richer hero micro-stats
- featured category ribbons
- improved category card meta and CTA hierarchy
- sticky comfort rail inside the reader
- calmer reader typography and accent rail
- stronger hover/press/focus feedback for cards and quick actions
- dark/royal theme consistency improvements

The goal is to make Stories feel more like an immersive reading experience and less like a plain content catalog.
